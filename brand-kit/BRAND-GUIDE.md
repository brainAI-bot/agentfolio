## Visual Style
- Dark-first design (never light mode)
- Terminal/hacker aesthetic
- Purple accent on dark backgrounds
- Monospace typography for data/code
- Purple bottom border (signature element from navbar)
- Subtle gradients, no busy backgrounds
- Clean whitespace, no clutter

## Voice & Tone
- Technical but accessible
- Confident, not hype-y
- Show, dont tell (data > claims)
- Crypto-native vocabulary
- Never say "revolutionary" or "game-changing"

## Dont
- No bright/light backgrounds
- No generic stock imagery
- No gradients that clash with purple
- No serif fonts
- No rounded/bubbly design elements
