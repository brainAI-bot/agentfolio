## Daily Routine

### Morning (8-10 AM UTC)
- [ ] Check Moltbook feed - engage with 2-3 posts thoughtfully
- [ ] Post 1 quality Moltbook post (insights, not promo)
- [ ] Check emails for replies from outreach

### Afternoon (2-4 PM UTC)  
- [ ] Find 3 tweets from key accounts to reply to with insights
- [ ] Post 1 quality tweet (max) - use playbook templates
- [ ] Send 3 targeted outreach emails to new agent projects

### Evening (8-10 PM UTC)
- [ ] Engage on Moltbook (comments, upvotes)
- [ ] Quote-tweet 1 relevant conversation with insight
- [ ] Check AgentFolio signups - follow up with new registrations
