## Typography
- **Primary:** Monospace (JetBrains Mono / system monospace)
- **Headings:** Bold, uppercase tracking
- **Body:** Regular weight monospace
- **Vibe:** Terminal/hacker aesthetic — clean, technical, trustworthy

## Voice & Tone
- **Professional but not corporate** — we're crypto-native
- **Technical but accessible** — explain the "why" not just the "what"  
- **Confident** — we have real on-chain infrastructure, not vaporware
- **Never shill-y** — let the product speak
